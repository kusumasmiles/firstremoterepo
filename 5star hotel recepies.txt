Here is the recipe to make the perfect tea latte:)....

Ingredients-
1. cinnamon
2. cardamom
3. ginger
4. nutmeg
5. star anise (optional)
6. cloves
7. sweetner (sugar)
8. black tea bags

Procedure
mix 1/2 cup water and 1/2 cup milk 
mix all the ingredients in it

And your yummy tea latte is ready
